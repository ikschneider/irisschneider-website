Speaking

I offer keynote presentations on the science of effective decision-making, covering a wide range of topics, including cognitive biases, heuristics, decision-making under uncertainty, benefits of ambivalence and more. I provide my audience with recommendations that are insightful and actionable, providing practical strategies that can be implemented to enhance decision-making effectiveness.

I also offer coaching, mentoring, and advice on the challenges and opportunities in academia, drawing on my training as a psychologist and my experiences as a first-generation student and female academic across countries and cultures.
